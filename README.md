# Privacy Policy — Alps Summits

**Last updated: 31 May 2026**

Alps Summits ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app
does — and does not — do with information.

## Summary

**Alps Summits does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.** There are no accounts,
no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

## Information stored on your device

Everything you create in the app — the summits you mark as climbed, dates, ratings and notes —
is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's on-device database (SwiftData). This
information never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. Deleting the app
removes this data.

## Purchases

Alps Summits offers an optional one-time "Pro" purchase and an optional tip. These are processed
entirely by **Apple** through the App Store. We never receive or store your payment details.
Your purchase entitlement is verified on your device by Apple's StoreKit; we operate no servers.

## Maps

The map is provided by **Apple Maps (MapKit)**. When you use the map, Apple may process map and
location requests in accordance with [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
Alps Summits does not request your location and does not track where you are.

## Summit data

Summit names, elevations and coordinates are adapted from **Wikipedia**, available under the
Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike license. This is public reference data and contains no
information about you.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Your control

Because all your data stays on your device, you are always in control. To erase everything,
delete the app from your device.
